Plant your feet on the floor, drop your gaze, and with a Palo Santo stick in hand, tap into the history of this holy wood and flow.

  • 5 sticks in one package
  • Helping your practice of yoga and meditation
  • Cleaning your air with healing smell
  • Allergie free
  • 30 days money back guarantee

How to use:

Light the end of the stick until there is a small flame. we recommend using a candle.
Once lit, let the Palo Santo burn for 10-20 seconds then blow out so only smoke remains. Then use the smoke to cleanse your space by painting the air with it.
As with any flame, or lit item, take caution when burning.
